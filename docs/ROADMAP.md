## Implementation Notes

### Tool Development Priorities
1. **Quick Wins** - Tools similar to existing ones (JSON to C#, JSON to XML)
2. **High Value** - Excel converters require xlsx library but have strong volume
3. **Completeness** - Filling gaps in conversion pairs (JSON‚ÜîXML, JSON‚ÜîExcel)

### Content Strategy
1. **Error articles** convert well - users have immediate problems to solve
2. **How-to guides** build authority and attract backlinks
3. **Comparison articles** capture decision-stage traffic
4. **Language guides** expand audience to specific developer communities

### Internal Linking Strategy
Every new piece of content should link to:
- At least 2 relevant tools
- At least 2 related articles
- The main validator (/)

Every new tool page should include:
- 800-1,200 words of educational content
- Links to related converters
- Links to relevant learn articles

## Quick Reference: Keyword Research Sources

When adding new tools/content, check these keyword patterns:
- "[format] to json" / "json to [format]"
- "json [error message]"
- "how to [action] json"
- "json [language]"
- "json vs [alternative]"
- "[format] validator online"

Use Ahrefs Keywords Explorer with filters:
- Volume: 100+
- KD: < 30 (prefer < 15)
- Include: "json"
